Privacy Policy

At Farm Hotel Link, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform. By accessing our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to ensure a seamless experience for farmers and hotel owners:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and address. Account credentials, including login details.
  • Business Information: Farm details (e.g., crops, location) for farmers. Hotel details (e.g., special dishes, location) for hotel owners.
  • Transaction Information: Order history, payment details, and order status.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies for analytics and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate transactions between farmers and hotel owners.
  • To personalize and improve your experience on the platform.
  • To send important updates, notifications, and promotional offers.
  • To maintain platform security and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: For payment processing, analytics, or customer support.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business Partners: To improve the services offered through our platform.

4. How We Protect Your Information

  • Secure servers and encryption for sensitive data.
  • Regular security audits and system updates.
  • Restricted access to personal data for authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Update: Review or update your personal details through your account.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Data Deletion: Request the deletion of your account and personal information.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails or offers.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve your experience and gather analytical data. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the platform may be limited.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the platform.
