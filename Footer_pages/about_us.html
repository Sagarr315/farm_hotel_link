Farm Hotel Link

About Us

Welcome to Farm Hotel Link, a platform designed to connect farmers and hotel owners directly, fostering a sustainable and mutually beneficial relationship.

At Farm Hotel Link, we understand the importance of fresh, high-quality ingredients for hotels and the need for farmers to get fair value for their hard work. Our mission is to bridge the gap between these two vital sectors, creating a platform where trust, transparency, and quality thrive.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the agricultural and hospitality industries by creating a seamless, efficient, and transparent marketplace that benefits both farmers and hotel owners.

Our Mission

  • Empower farmers by providing direct access to hotels and fair pricing for their produce.
  • Enable hotels to source fresh and high-quality ingredients directly from trusted farmers.
  • Promote sustainable farming and local produce to enhance community well-being.

What We Offer

For Farmers:

  • A platform to showcase their crops and products.
  • Direct communication with hotel owners to establish trustworthy relationships.
  • Tools to manage orders and track transactions effectively.

For Hotel Owners:

  • Access to a wide range of fresh, locally grown produce.
  • A system to track orders and maintain transparency in transactions.
  • Options to showcase their hotel and special dishes, boosting marketing efforts.

Why Choose Us?

  • Transparency: We ensure clear communication and fair transactions between farmers and hotel owners.
  • Simplicity: Our user-friendly platform makes the process easy and efficient for both parties.
  • Sustainability: Supporting local produce and reducing dependency on intermediaries promote sustainable practices.
  • Growth-Oriented: Helping both farmers and hotels grow their businesses through direct collaboration.

How It Works

  1. Farmers register and showcase their produce on our platform.
  2. Hotel Owners browse through the available products and place their orders.
  3. Both parties can track the order status, ensuring transparency and smooth transactions.

Join Us Today!

Whether you’re a farmer looking for a reliable marketplace or a hotel owner seeking the freshest ingredients, Farm Hotel Link is here to cater to your needs. Together, let’s build a stronger, more sustainable food ecosystem.
